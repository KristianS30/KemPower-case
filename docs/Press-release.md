# RoomLight — The last room light controller your hotel needs!

Prism AB just released their latest product called RoomLight. It is a hotel room light controller that allows you to sync your custom lighting settings to every room.

Designing lighting for your hotel can take weeks. RoomLight makes it a one day job! Also, if you ever want to make any changes — big or small — you can apply them to every room at once!

_“As a lighting company, we know how challenging it can be to manage lighting on a larger scale. RoomLight makes it simple and efficient — we’ve actually been using it in our own offices for over a year now,”_ said the Product Manager at Prism AB.

RoomLight is the easiest solution to managing your hotel's lighting. Prism AB will send a representative to install all the equipment needed, after which you are all set! Your staff will also get free training on how to use the controller to make sure you can make the best of it!

_"The everchanging amount of natural light here in the north made managing our lighting very difficult. Now with RoomLight we have lighting settings for the whole year automatically changing with the seasons,"_ said the Lodging manager of Maple Hotels Ltd
